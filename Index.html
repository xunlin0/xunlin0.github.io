道生一，一生二，二生三，三生万物 --- 《道德经》.

Dao generates One, One generates Two, Two generates Three, Three generates everything. --- 《Dao Te Ching》.

I am a student at Tsinghua University (YMSC), Beijing, China (PRC). My supervisor is Professor William Donovan.

My research is in algebraic geometry. I am interested in the geometry of derived categories, especially the indecomposability problems. I am also interested in Hochschild (co)homology theories, para-canonical systems, Hodge theory, and rational points on plane curves.
